Non-Government Children Homes

                In Tamil Nadu, Children Homes are functioning under the provisions of Juvenile Justice Act, 2015 to cater to the needs of children requiring care and protection. 10 Homes are functioning under the control of Department of Social Defence, 26 under the Department of Social Welfare and 148 Homes under the NGOs with grants-in-aid from the Government.

               The State Government supports Non-Governmental Organizations which run child care institutions by providing grants-in-aid of Rs.2000/- per child per month under ICPS towards the maintenance of children apart from bedding, rent, water, electricity, transportation, salary for staff and other contingencies. Children Homes under Bala Vihar (Boys and Girls) and Sree Arunodayam in Chennai, Children’s Home for Girls under Tere-Des-Home Trust, Tiruvanamalai and Arivagam at Mayiladuthurai are for children with special needs.

               The Children are provided with basic needs of food (including weekly once Mutton, weekly thrice Egg/Banana), clothing and bedding (including 4 sets of Uniforms, 2 sets of Casuals and 2 sets of Chappals) and Dormitory with adequate facilities.
