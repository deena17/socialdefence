Juvenile Justice Boards

               As per section 4 of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2000, Juvenile Justice Boards have been constituted in all the 32 Districts of the State. These Boards are dealing with the cases relating to the Juvenile in Conflict Law.

               Juvenile Justice Board consists of a Metropolitan magistrate or a Judicial Magistrate of the First Class with two Social Worker Members of whom one should be a women.

               These Boards hold sittings three days in a week on Monday, Wednesday and Friday in the premises of the respective Observation Homes / Notified places.

A sum of Rs. 285.10 lakh has been provided in the Budget Estimate 2015-2016 for the effective running of Juvenile Justice Boards.
